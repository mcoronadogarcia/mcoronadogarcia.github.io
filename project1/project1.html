Parallel Coordinates for Colorado County Education Level between 1970 to 2016.

The map you're looking at displays the percent of education attainment in Colorado counties. This information is displayed in a multivariate manner. The map was inspired by the somehwat-common population prymaids from various sources online. Unfortunately while the prymaid allows you a finer grained analysis, the form doesn't lend itself to integrating more than one country at a time. The parallel coordinates and map combination neatly follows Scheiderman's mantra.

  • P.C. enables you (the user) an overview of all the data. About 64 colorado counties are depicted, instead of the P.P.'s 1.
  • There are three primary points of interaction; brushing P.C. axes, the dropdown attribute selection, and the map hovering. Each of them aid in assisting the user to get the data they need by zooming and filtering the data.
  • The map tooltip interaction allows precise information, thus serving as a proxy for details on demand.

Variable Description: All variables are describing the percent of adults in a county with a specific education attainment.

  • LHS: stands for less than a high school diploma.
  • HS: stands for a high school diploma
  • SC: stands for some college education
  • C: stands for college education
  • 1970 depicts the year 1970
  • 1980 depicts the year 1980
  • 1990 depicts the year 1990
  • 2000 depicts the year 2000
  • 1216 depicts the year 2012 to 2016
